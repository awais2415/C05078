Our Story

Hunza Haven Guest House was opened by the Baig family in 1999. We are based in Karimabad, Hunza Valley, at 2,438 metres above sea level.

Our building is made using traditional Hunza stone and wood. Baltit Fort and the main bazaar are just a short walk away.

25+ Years Open
1500+ Guests
12 Rooms

Location

We are in Gilgit-Baltistan, northern Pakistan

Getting Here

Fly to Gilgit Airport from Islamabad (45 minutes). We offer a free pickup if booked in advance.

Best Time to Visit

Spring (April to May) for apricot blossoms. Summer (June to September) for trekking and clear skies.

Nearby Attractions

Baltit Fort, Altit Fort, Attabad Lake and Eagle's Nest viewpoint are all within easy reach.
