Common Questions

Things guests often ask us before visiting

Do I need a permit?

Most of Hunza Valley does not require a permit. Some border areas do and we can help arrange this.

Is Wi-Fi available?

Yes, we have free Wi-Fi. Speeds can vary in the mountains but it works for browsing and messaging.

What currency is used?

Pakistani Rupees (PKR). ATMs are in Karimabad town. We do not accept card payments currently.
